About MediCo

MediCo is a smart OP ticket management system designed to simplify and fully digitalize patient registration and token handling inside hospitals.

The system automatically generates unique patient IDs, assigns department-based token numbers, and securely organizes patient data. This reduces manual workload and minimizes waiting time for patients.

MediCo supports multiple hospitals through a centralized Super Admin module. Hospital Admins can view and manage department-wise registrations, patient lists, and daily analytics, all from a clean and simple interface.

The platform is built to provide a seamless experience with modern UI, fast performance, and a structured workflow — ensuring accuracy, transparency, and efficient OP operations.
